About Atmos

Atmos foundation is a non governmental foundation aimed to control and combat against negative climate changes and it's effects on living organisms but yet to positively control the environment and it's natural resources for a better life. Atmos foundation is to collaborate and work with local and international partners concerning environments, population, public health and agricultural services. We believe in control and changing the climate positively. It is founded by Kondwani Matuwamba.
